Peshawar, Pakistan BS COMPUTER SCIENCE IELTS

I'm an AI researcher with a deep interest in Deep Learning, Medical Image Analysis, and Explainable AI (XAI). My core focus lies in building and interpreting neural networks that operate on complex medical data — particularly MRI-based classification tasks for neurodegenerative disease detection.

I recently co-authored a Q1 journal paper on Multi-view Deep Learning for Early Alzheimer's Detection published in Image and Vision Computing (IF 4.2), where I worked on multi-modal MRI classification to detect early progression from Mild Cognitive Impairment (MCI) to Alzheimer's Disease — combining deep feature extraction with explainability techniques to make model decisions interpretable for clinical use.

I'm fascinated by the intersection of Multimodal AI, Large Language Models (LLMs), and Medical Imaging — exploring how vision-language models and multimodal architectures can enhance diagnostic reasoning beyond what single-modality systems can achieve. I am eager to pursue a Master's degree to push the boundaries of Medical XAI, multimodal classification, and clinically trustworthy AI systems.
